How the Queue System Works

1
Generate Your Token

Visit the Get Queue Token portal, select your service office. A unique token number is issued instantly.

2
Wait Comfortably

Monitor the Public Display board or listen for your number to be called at your assigned window.

3
Proceed to Your Window

When called, proceed promptly. Assistance is available for persons with disabilities and special needs.

4
Rate Your Experience

After being served, share feedback to help us improve service delivery for everyone.
